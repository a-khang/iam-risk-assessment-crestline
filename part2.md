### 2.1.2 Document mover role transition process

### 2.1.3 Document leaver deprovisioning process

## 2.2 Access Request and Approval
_Defines how identities obtain access beyond their baseline permissions, especially in role-based contexts. Additional and elevated access is granted intentionally, reviewed with a view to risk, and does not persist infinitely._
### 2.2.1 Define access request triggers and inputs
_Certain criteria must be met, and required details must be provided, in access requests to make an informed decision on whether to approve or deny._
Requests may be made when:
- access is needed but the privileges are not included in the base entitlements for a role;
- access is needed at a higher sensitivity or tier of risk than their typical role;
- access is needed for temporary assignments or projects.

Access administrators will need to know (responding to the request)
- the resource or system requested;
- what kind of access the requester needs;
- business justification describing why access is required;
- requested duration.
